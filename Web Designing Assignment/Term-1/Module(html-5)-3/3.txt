Semantic element in HTML5?
- The meaning of Semantic is the meaning,
i.e. to give meaning to any word, it means what is the meaning of a word,
its called Semantic.
- A Semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the brouser
  and the developer.
- The non Semantic element tells nothing about its content like <div> and
  <span> element.
- Example of Semantic elements like <form>, <table>, and <article> - 
  clearly defines its content.
- As soon as you read this element you will know that the usage.
- Semantic elements: <article>,<aside>,<detials>,<header>,<footer>,
  <mean>,<mark>,<section>,<summery>,<time>,<nav> etc.
