What is difference between HTML and XHTML?
1. HTML:-
- stands for HyperText Markup Language.
- Devloped By Tim Berners-Lee in 1991.
- Ext. .html or .htm
- tag is not mandatory means <html>, <head>, <title> and <body>
- closing is not mandatory
2. XHTML:-
- stands for Extensible HyperText Markup Language.
- Devloped By W3C Organization in 2000.
- .xhtml, .xht, .xml, .htm or .htm or .html
- tag is mandatory means <html>, <head>, <title> and <body>
- closing is mandatory
