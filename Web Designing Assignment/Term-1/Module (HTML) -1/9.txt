How is Cell Padding different from Cell Spacing?
- Cell Padding and Cell Spacing the difference between both is in below details.
A. Cell Padding:-
- The row of the table or the <td> tag of the table is the space between
  the cell ball or boundary and its contents i .e Cell Padding
  or
- Cell padding specifies the space between the table boundry and the cell 
  content.

B. Cell Spacing:-
- Cell Spacing specifies the space between the table cells 
  or
- Cell Spacing controls the space between table cells
